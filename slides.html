Guiding principals

  • One consistent experience is more important than any one feature.
  • Features shipped to all platforms near simultaneously.
  • Graceful degradation of features due to device, service and network capabilities.
  • Client should be as simple as possible.
  • Apply DRY principle - Do not Repeat Yourself.
  • Open development - easily extensible.
  • No one person can be an expert on everything.
  • Reduce dependencies to increase velocity.
